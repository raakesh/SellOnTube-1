## North Star
**Goal:** Drive high-intent organic traffic from B2B founders, SaaS operators, and service businesses evaluating YouTube for customer acquisition and lead generation.

**SellonTube offers:** content strategy, YouTube SEO, microtools, and a premium done-for-you agency service.

**Not for:** YouTubers, creators, influencers, or hobbyists.
